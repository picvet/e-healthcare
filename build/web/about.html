WHAT DO WE DO?


We help connect people with doctors all over the world.



HOW DO WE CONNECT YOU WITH DOCTORS?


We have an online chatting in our web application
All you have to do is go on to our home page then
select register as patient, enter your details on
following page. Then you're all set.

You can start a chat with any online doctor so
you can find the help you need.

HOW DO I REGISTER AS A DOCTOR?


You just have to contact our admin so that we can
verify that you're really a doctor.
Then you can create your own account as well.
